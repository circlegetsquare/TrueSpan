# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Basketball enthusiasts and engaged fans who want to explore NBA Draft Combine data beyond the surface level. They know what wingspan means and why it matters — they're reading Cleaning the Glass, not watching SportsCenter. They come with a specific question ("how long is this year's class compared to 2017?") and want the data to answer it quickly, without friction or onboarding.

## Product Purpose

True Size makes a prospect's physical profile legible at a glance. It collapses height, wingspan, and standing reach into one number — functional height — so fans can compare prospects across eras without doing the math themselves. Success looks like a fan loading it during combine weekend and walking away with a real, grounded opinion on a prospect's physical profile.

## Brand Personality

Clean, minimal, open. The tool earns trust through restraint. It doesn't dress up or editorialize the data. Voice is direct and confident without being loud — the numbers speak; the UI steps back.

## Anti-references

- **ESPN / mainstream sports UI**: loud accent colors, score-ticker energy, red-and-black aggression, information density used as a status signal.
- **Generic SaaS analytics**: navy sidebar, enterprise dashboard vibes, bar charts for the sake of bar charts.
- **Fantasy sports apps**: gamified UI, badge-heavy, leaderboard mentality — this is exploration, not competition.

## Design Principles

1. **Data before decoration.** Every visual element earns its place by making numbers easier to read or compare. Styling that competes with data is cut.
2. **Trust through restraint.** Credibility comes from what's left out. No styling where a plain number does the job better.
3. **Obvious is good.** Basketball fans aren't here to learn a new UI paradigm. Filters, comparisons, and the scatter plot should work exactly as expected.
4. **Spatial reasoning made visible.** The metric is about physical space; the interface should honor that — honest scale, no decoration that distorts the data's proportions.
5. **Speed of insight.** From load to "I understand this player's profile" in one glance. No onboarding required.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Best-effort accessibility. Prioritize readable contrast (4.5:1 for body text, 3:1 for large text) and full keyboard navigability. No formal compliance target.
